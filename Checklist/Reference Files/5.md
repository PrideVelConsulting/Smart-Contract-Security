
## 5) ++i costs less gas than i++, especially when it's used in for-loops (same for decrement operator too)


---

### **Category Type** : GAS OPTIMIZATION


### **Explanation** :

```javascript

 //Unoptimized
  for (uint256 i = 0; i < total; i++) {

 //Optimized
  for (uint256 i = 0; i < total; ++i) {


```
