##   Declarations of array variables should not have a space between the type and the brackets.


---

### **Category Type** : QUALITY ASSURANCE / NON-CRITICAL


### **Explanation** : 


```javascript

//Yes:
   	    
   		uint[] x;
   	    
 //No:
   	    
   		uint [] x;
   	


```
